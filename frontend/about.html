About the Platform

HH Port Analytics is a maritime intelligence platform developed to provide visibility into global port operations and logistics performance.

The system enables users to monitor vessel arrivals, berthing activities, waiting times, and operational delays through an interactive map-based dashboard.

By combining real-time analytics with historical operational data, the platform helps shipping companies, logistics providers, and port authorities make informed decisions and improve supply chain efficiency.

The dashboard has been designed with a focus on usability, performance, and modern maritime visualization techniques to support data-driven port management.
